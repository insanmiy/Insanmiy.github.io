Troop Information

Boy Scout Troop 433 meets at St. Andrew's Episcopal Church and has proudly served the community since 1988. Our troop gathers every Monday evening from 7:00 PM to 8:30 PM, providing a consistent and welcoming environment for scouts to learn, grow, and lead.

Troop 433 is a scout led troop that empowers scouts to take responsibility, develop leadership skills, and gain confidence through hands on experiences.
